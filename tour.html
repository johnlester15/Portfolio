Tour Days Breakdown

DAY 1

UP IT Center Cebu — First Day of Exploration

Our tour at the UP IT Center Cebu highlighted the fusion of IT, marketing, and business. We learned how tech companies integrate digital marketing strategies and develop innovative business models to drive growth in the digital economy.

Key Observations & Learnings
  • Explored marketing strategies in tech businesses
  • Learned about business models in the IT sector
  • Gained insights into branding and customer engagement
DAY 2

Cebu — Visit to Dynata & Rivant IT

On the second day, we visited Dynata and Rivant IT in Cebu. At Dynata, we learned about the wide range of career opportunities in IT across different fields. At Rivant IT, we focused on networking and its crucial role in modern tech infrastructure.

Key Observations & Learnings
  • Learned about the diverse career paths in IT at Dynata
  • Explored the importance of networking in tech at Rivant IT
  • Gained insights into network security and infrastructure management
  • Discovered the growing role of networking in global connectivity
DAY 3

Cebu — Mata Technologies

On the third day, we visited Mata Technologies, where we explored the fascinating world of virtual tours and 360-degree technology. We saw how these innovations are transforming industries like real estate, tourism, and education. The experience wrapped up our exploration in Cebu, emphasizing the role of immersive technologies in shaping the future.

Key Observations & Learnings
  • Discovered the power of virtual tours and 360-degree technology in various industries
  • Learned how immersive experiences are enhancing customer engagement and business opportunities
  • Explored how Mata Technologies is pushing the boundaries of virtual reality applications
  • Understood the growing demand for virtual solutions in the global market
DAY 4

Bohol — Tarsier 117 & Cultural Experience

On the final day, we visited Tarsier 117 in Bohol, where we learned about their use of CCTV systems and technologies for emergency response and location tracking. We also explored the island's natural wonders and cultural heritage, understanding how technology is being used to improve safety and efficiency in rural areas.

Key Observations & Learnings
  • Learned how CCTV and emergency location systems are improving safety in rural areas
  • Explored how technology helps in real-time monitoring for quicker response times
  • Gained insights into the importance of tech in community safety and disaster preparedness
  • Understood how these tech solutions support sustainable development in Bohol
